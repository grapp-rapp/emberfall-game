❓ How to Play

Goal: Clear each world's mission to summon its boss. Beat the boss to expand the world and unlock the next — across three worlds: The Valley (Leviathan) → The Wastes (Iron Colossus) → Emberpeak (Fire Lord).

💡 Tips
• Heal up: stay out of danger for a few seconds and you slowly regenerate health — taking a hit resets it.
• Boss fights: regular enemies thin out while a boss is alive, so focus the boss. Watch for red telegraphs and dodge.
• Spend coins in the shop (TAB in-game, or 🛒 from the menu) on weapons, magic, tunnels, ship, and cosmetic skins.
• Beating a boss pays a big coin bonus — gear up before the next world.
