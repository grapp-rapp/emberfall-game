Privacy Policy — Emberfall

Last updated: 22 June 2026

Short version: Emberfall does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Your game progress is saved only on your own device. Nothing is sent to us or to anyone else.

Information we collect

None. Emberfall does not ask for, collect, or transmit any personal data. We do not use accounts, sign-in, analytics, advertising identifiers, or tracking of any kind.

Data stored on your device

To let you continue where you left off, the game saves your progress (coins, upgrades, skins, campaign progress, settings, and best scores) in your device's local browser storage (localStorage). This data never leaves your device, is not readable by us, and is removed if you uninstall the app or clear its data. You can also wipe it in-game via Settings → Reset Progress.

Internet & third parties

The game runs entirely offline — all of its code and assets are bundled in the app. We do not share data with third parties, because we do not collect any. The only optional outbound link is a "Donate" button that opens PayPal in your browser if you choose to tap it; we receive no personal information through it.

Children

Emberfall is a fantasy combat game suitable for general audiences. Because it collects no personal information from anyone, it collects none from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
